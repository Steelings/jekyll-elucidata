The Elucidata Community

is initiated by the EluciDATA Lab of Sirris

It gathers a network of companies with an interest in data innovation and AI. Its main objective is to provide a platform for knowledge transfer, networking, and collaboration.

Benefits to participation

Gain insights in emerging technologies through comprehensible industrial use cases on how to successfully apply AI and data science techniques in several sectors

Access to our two annual Community events

  • EluciDATA Brokerage - "Data and AI innovation" offering the opportunity for networking, learning about or pitching new R&D initiatives, forming new R&D partnerships and following keynote presentations on emerging technology trends.
  • EluciDATA Tech Talk addressing industrial deployment of data innovation and AI, showcasing recent success stories and realised proof-of-concepts on real-world use cases, and exchanging technological know-how and business insights with peers. Topics from the last editions include: "How to fully exploit the data generated by your assets?", The relevance of data security and data privacy in an industrial context", "Are advanced AI methods ready to be applied in industry?".
